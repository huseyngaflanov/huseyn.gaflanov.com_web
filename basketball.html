Career Path

The Journey

2019
Origin
Baku, Azerbaijan — Early Career
Identified at a young age as a standout talent by the head coach of Azerbaijan's national youth program, Tahir Bakhshiyev. Began training seriously as a big man with the ambition to develop a versatile game.
2021
Europe
Real Madrid, Valencia Basket – Clubs in Spain.
Tryouts in top spanish clubs with top level trainers. An early international step that exposed me to elite European basketball development.
2022
Europe
Lithuania — NBA Academy School
At age 14, became one of the few Azerbaijani players to train at the NBA Academy program in Lithuania. Full season with the local team against high level competition.
2023
USA
Southern California Academy, Reseda CA
Sent to the United States at 15 with support from the Azerbaijan Basketball Federation. Trained at the Southern California Academy (SCA) — elite skill development, athletic performance programs, and exposure to top American coaching.
2024
National Team
FIBA U16 EuroBasket 2024 — Division C
Represented Azerbaijan at the U16 European Championship. Scoring 9.6 PPG and 8.4 RPG.
2025
3x3
U17 3x3 National Team — III CIS Games
Part of the U-17 Azerbaijani 3x3 squad at the CIS Games. The team defeated Tajikistan and competed against multiple nations, with Gaflanov contributing on both ends of the floor.
2025
Senior
Azerbaijan Senior National Team Call-Up
At 16, called up to the senior national team squad for FIBA Basketball World Cup 2027 European Pre-Qualifiers. Azerbaijan faced Kosovo (away) and Ireland (home) — one of the youngest players in the national program.
Now
Present
Sabah Basketball Club · ADA University
Playing professionally for Sabah Basketball Club in the Azerbaijani ABL while pursuing a bachelor's degree at ADA University in Baku. Eyes set on Division I college basketball in the United States and a long-term professional career.
